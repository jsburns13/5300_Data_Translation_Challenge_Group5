Research question and data summary

The second research question is investigating if retail employment differed from other industries. Several data transformations were generated to compare:

  1. Industry groupings based on the IND1990 categories found here: https://cps.ipums.org/cps-action/variables/IND1990#codes_section
  2. Filtering out Not In Universe and Military jobs to focus on the private sector
  3. A binary variable that is TRUE when the industry is retail, and FALSE otherwise
  4. Aggregated monthly employment rates by industry
  5. Since the lock down began mid-March, all data from March 2020 is filtered out to prevent issues due to reporting inconsistencies

For reference, the average employment rates by industry are as follows:

Finally, individual models were created for each industry to demonstrate individual comparisons. Additional work could be conducted to precisely identify the significance of differences between pairs of industries, but the conclusion above was determined to sufficiently answer the research question at hand.

IndList <- split(Q2_data_mo, f=Q2_data_mo$Industry)
model_list <- list()
rdrobust_list <- list()

for (i in 1:length(IndList)) {
  indus <- levels(IndList[[i]]$Industry)[i]
  modelname <- paste0(indus, "_model") %>%
    replace(" ", "_")
  
  model_list[[paste0(indus)]] <- assign(modelname,feols(Employment ~ weeks * disc_id, data=IndList[[i]]))
  rdrobust_list[[paste0(indus)]] <- assign(modelname,rdrobust(IndList[[i]]$Employment,IndList[[i]]$weeks,c=0,p=2,h=25,kernel="uniform"))
}
